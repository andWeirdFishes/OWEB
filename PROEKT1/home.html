Our History


The story of Mean Green Mystery Machines begins in the early 1980s, when a small garage in the heart of the city of Las Veles was home to two best friends, Mile and Gjore, who shared an obsession with rare, vintage cars. They were passionate about restoring old vehicles and giving them a fresh life, but what set them apart was their ability to uncover the most mysterious and unique carsâ€”vehicles with stories that no one else could unravel.

Word spread quickly about the duo's ability to find and bring back to life cars thought to be lost forever. Soon, their tiny garage was transformed into a well-known shop, attracting car enthusiasts from all over. They quickly became known as the "Mean Green Mystery Machines," a name inspired by their signature lime-green psychedelic logo and their knack for discovering rare, quirky cars that had been hidden away for decades.

Over the years, Mean Green Mystery Machines grew, expanding their inventory and their reputation. Today, they continue to honor their roots by focusing on both new and classic cars, ensuring that every vehicle they offer has a story to tell. The garage may have turned into a full-scale dealership, but Mile and Gjore's passion for uncovering rare gems and reviving the past is as strong as ever.
